Wisconsin Card Sorting Test

PsyToolkit JS Implementation

Instructions (1/3)

You will see four target cards on the right side of the screen.

A new card will appear at the top of the screen on every turn.

Instructions (2/3)

Your task is to match the top card to one of the four target cards by tapping your choice.

The app will tell you if your match is correct or wrong.

Instructions (3/3)

The matching rule might change without warning during the test.

Use the feedback to figure out the current correct rule.
